Fried Rice

Why is it so special & How to make the best fried rice

Fried rice is a perfectly balanced dish with proper mix-ins. This dish can be made very quickly and can be stored in the fridge/frozen for later use. It is also a very versatile dish because we can change the ingredients as per our mood and taste. It is one of the few dishes that even with minimum ingredients it tastes so good. Making fried rice is a breeze with less and simple ingredients.
Just Preheat your pan before swirling in oil and aromatics, then add uncooked proteins if applicable. Add in rice and vegetables, vigorously tossing ingredients for that toasty, golden-brown color that signals big flavor.

Best ways to make it

Wok:
This ancient cooking vessel is the most commonly used (and convenient) method for cooking fried rice.
Pan:
A basic frying pan or skillet can also produce tasty fried rice in a pinch. Approach this method the same way you would a wok â€” high heat is key.
Griddle:
Start by sauteing the vegetables and aromatics, then add cooked rice and eggs to another part of the griddle's surface for a quick and satisfying meal.
Instand Pot:
Use the Instant Pot's saute mode to get the garlic to a golden-brown color, then stir in rice, veggies, and eggs. Mix well and serve piping hot.

    The Key Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vegetables or Chicken or Egg
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Sauces
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 2 Cup (cooked)
Sesame Oil 3 Tsp
Garlic 1 Tbsp (minced)
Soy Sauce 3 Tbsp
Scallions 4 (thinly sliced)
Mushrooms 1/2 Cup
Vegetables 1/2 Cup
Koshar Salt As per requirement

Directions

  1. Assemble the ingrdients.
  2. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in the oil, then saute garlic and add the vegetables, mushrooms cook them al dante on high.
  3. Add the Scallions, required salt etc
  4. Add Soy sauce and stirr it well for few seconds.
  5. Finally add the cooked rice to it and stirr for few minutes.
  6. Serve hot and Enjoy.
